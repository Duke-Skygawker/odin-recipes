Peanut Butter Protein Milkshake


Description

Healthy snack or breakfast that will take care of all your protein needs!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup chocolate almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons powdered peanut butter (such as PB2®)
  • 1 tablespoon cacao powder
  • ¼ cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • ½ cup ice

Steps

  1. Combine chocolate almond milk, peanut butter powder, and cacao powder in a high-speed blender (such as Vitamixu0026reg;). Add black beans and ice. Secure lid into place. Blend on speed variable 1, slowly moving up to the top speed. Blend for 1 minute until ice is incorporated.
  2. Pour milkshake into a tall glass.
